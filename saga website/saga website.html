YHS SAGA

 Yukon High School's Sexuality and Gender Alliance

We will put forth advocacy for the voices of LGBTQIA+ identifying people and end all forms of hatred in our hallways and classrooms. We want to show that SAGA is not just a safe space for LGBTQIA+, but for straight allies as well.

ABOUT

Who : SAGA is welcome to anyone who would like to come.

What : safe space for LGBTQIA+ students and allies working to educate peers.

When : wednesdays during Rethink.

Where : meetings are held in S134 and N142. check @yhssaga on twitter for updates.
